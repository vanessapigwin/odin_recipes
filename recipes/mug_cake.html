Chocolate Mug Cake

Description

This easy mug cake is easy to make with basic ingredients and comes together quickly in the microwave. What’s not to love?

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon baking soda
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt together in a large microwave-safe mug; stir in milk, canola oil, water, and vanilla extract.
  2. Cook in the microwave until cake is done in the middle, about 1 minute 45 seconds.
  3. Enjoy!
